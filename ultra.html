Precision dual-frequency GPS.
Apple Watch Ultra 2 integrates L1 and L5 GPS into
an antenna design that provides greater range with
incredible power efficiency. It combines data from
both frequencies to provide amazingly accurate
distance, pace and route calculations, with a more
consistent signal.

Unmatched accuracy in dense urban environments.
The dual-frequency system combines with Apple Maps
to better identify actual locations. Accuracy is boosted by
satellite and signal models. A custom, advanced algorithm
makes optimal use of available satellite signals. And
on-device machine learning intelligently caches map data
for improved accuracy.
